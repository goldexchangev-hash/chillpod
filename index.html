Good to know

Will it fit my phone?
The slot fits most standard and large phones, including slim cases. Add your exact maximum dimensions here once finalized.
How long does it stay cold?
A frozen core keeps a phone cool through a long outing. Between trips, pop it back in the freezer — it's reusable indefinitely.
Is it okay around sand and water?
It's one solid, wipe-clean piece built for the beach bag and cooler. Rinse off sand, and dry it before refreezing.
What about condensation on my phone?
Like any cold item brought into warm air, it can pick up a little condensation — just wipe the phone when you take it out. Best in dry heat; avoid steam rooms.
How do I refreeze it?
Just place it back in the freezer between uses. No refills needed unless you ever empty the core.
